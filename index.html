Hi! I'm Nicole

I'm a UX/UI Designer with a background in graphic design.

Designing Digital Experiences that Humans Love

You know that feeling you get when something works? It would not surprise me if you said no. Because there should not be a special feeling when things work. It's what's expected. What if the products you use work well AND bring a certain sense of calm, joy or accomplishment?

Having a background in graphic design and marketing, I draw customers into a product or service with images and typography

As a UX/UI Designer, I seek to understand more about the people who use certain products and the problems that are unsolved. I design digital experiences with purpose — blending usefulness with aesthetics.
